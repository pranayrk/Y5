What is a Force?

Gravity is the force which pulls all objects towards the Earth

Force Diagrams

A normal force is a support force exerted by a body in contact with another

Only unbalanced forces cause motion

An applied force is a force that is applied to an object by a person or another object in contact

Satellites are objects that orbit around the Earth
