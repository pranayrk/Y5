Essay Writing

For the January homeroom activity
The essay should contain the following parts and should be atleast 150-200 words long.
  • Title: A title that describes what the essay is about
  • Paragraph 1: Explaining what resourcefulness is and discussing its importance.
  • Paragraph 2: Sharing an example (from the given list) and highlight the resourcefulness shown.
  • Conclusion: Highlight resourcefulness as a valuable skill and encourage readers to cultivate it.

### Examples of Resourcefulness * The inventions of **Leonardo da Vinci** * The Story of the **Trojan Horse** * The Story of **Wright Brothers**' First Flight * **Frank Morris**' Escape from Alcatraz * The improvised return of the **Apollo 13** space mission * The **Bottle School** project, Guatemala * **Earthships** by the Garbage Warrior, Michael Reynolds * How ancient Egyptians built the Pyramids * The **Recycled Orchestra**, Paraguay
